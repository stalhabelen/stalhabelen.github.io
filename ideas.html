Ideas

Rough notes on future projects, research directions, and questions I am thinking about.

Bridging Seismic Imaging and Neural Fields

Concept note

How far can NeRF-style models go for underground imaging when measurements are limited and noisy? I am interested in representations that respect underlying physics, not just black box models.

Meta RL for Real-world Skill Transfer

Concept note

Many reinforcement learning benchmarks assume idealized environments. I am curious about meta RL setups where tasks reflect real robotics skills that need fast adaptation under uncertainty and hardware limits.
