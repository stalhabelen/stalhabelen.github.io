Space Defense Technologies – SDT

Applied research internship · 2025

Exploratory work on bringing ideas from neural rendering and modern ML into wave-based sensing domains such as marine seismic acquisition and FMCW radar.

  • Neural Rendering and NeRF. Implemented a compact Neural Radiance Fields (NeRF) pipeline from scratch in PyTorch, using COLMAP for structure-from-motion. Investigated how multi-view consistency and volumetric rendering ideas could be adapted to seismic CMP gathers for subsurface imaging.
  • Radar Signal Processing. Developed a supervised CNN pipeline to classify objects (Drones, Cars, People) using range–Doppler maps from the Real Doppler RAD-DAR dataset, reaching about 93 percent test accuracy with careful preprocessing and regularisation.
  • Research directions. Explored speech-inspired tokenisation and sequence-style representations for detecting patterns in seismic signals, reusing ideas from automatic speech recognition and self-supervised audio models.

Related technical write-ups live on the seismic NeRF, radar classification, and SwinIR sections of the projects page.
