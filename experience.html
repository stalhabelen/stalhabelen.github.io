Applied Research Intern · Aug–Sep 2025

Conducted conceptual research on visualizing seismic data inspired by Neural Radiance Fields (NeRF), experimenting with 3D data representations and deep learning based approaches for subsurface imaging. Explored how NeRF-style models could be adapted from vision to wave-based sensing for underground layers. Worked with synthetic seismic-style data to think about reconstruction, noise, and limited measurements. Connected the internship work with my long-term SpeedNeRF project, which aims to bring NeRF ideas to sound waves.
